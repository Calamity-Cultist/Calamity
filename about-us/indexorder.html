Thai Long Tea is a traditional herbal tea originating from Thailand, renowned for its unique, aromatic blend of local herbs and plants. Often infused with ingredients like lemongrass, pandan leaves, and butterfly pea flowers, the tea is celebrated for its soothing properties, vivid colors, and health benefits, including improved digestion and relaxation. Its refreshing, slightly floral flavor and vibrant appearance make it a popular choice for both locals and tourists looking to experience authentic Thai flavors.
